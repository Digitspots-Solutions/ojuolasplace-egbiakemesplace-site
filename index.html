About Us

At Ojuolasplace & Egbiakemesplace, we are passionate about transforming ideas into stunning realities. Based in Lugbe, Abuja, our studio specializes in fashion design and creative services that reflect the vibrant spirit of Nigerian style. With years of experience and a commitment to quality craftsmanship, we work closely with each client to understand their needs and deliver designs that exceed expectations. Whether you need a show-stopping outfit for a special occasion, everyday wear with a unique twist, or complete creative design solutions for your brand, we're here to make it happen. Our attention to detail, personalized service, and dedication to excellence have made us a trusted name in Abuja's fashion community.
